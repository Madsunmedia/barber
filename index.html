Our Story

Built on Passion.
Defined by Craft.

Cut & Conquer was born from a simple belief: every man deserves to feel powerful. In 2016, our founder Marcus Ellis — a third-generation barber — opened our doors with a straight razor, a leather chair, and an unbreakable standard.

Today, we are the city's most trusted grooming destination. Our team of elite barbers have shaped the jawlines, confidence, and careers of over 12,000 clients — from entrepreneurs to athletes to everyday kings.

This isn't just a barbershop. This is where legends are crafted.

FAQ

Questions? We've Got You.

Use our online booking form below, call us directly, or DM us on Instagram. We're appointment-only to respect your time — book 24 hours in advance for the best availability.

A Signature Haircut takes 45–60 minutes. Beard Sculpting is 30–45 minutes. The Royal Razor Shave is 60 minutes. The Elite Package is a full 90-minute experience.

We exclusively use premium, skin-safe grooming products including American Crew, Uppercut Deluxe, and Baxter of California. All products are paraben-free and dermatologist approved.

We are primarily appointment-based to ensure every client gets our full attention. Same-day walk-ins may be accommodated based on availability — call ahead and we'll do our best.

We ask for 24-hour notice for cancellations. No-shows may be charged 50% of the service fee. Life happens — just let us know and we'll reschedule without any hassle.
